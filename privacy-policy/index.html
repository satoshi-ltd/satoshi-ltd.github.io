Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11/11/2024

At Satoshi Ltd, protecting the privacy of our users is our utmost priority. We have designed our applications to ensure your personal data remains under your complete control. This Privacy Policy outlines our approach to data protection and how our applications operate without compromising your privacy.

1. No Data Collection Policy

No Telemetry: Satoshi Ltd does not collect, track, or share user data. Our applications are entirely telemetry-free.

No Cloud Storage: All data generated or managed within our applications is stored exclusively on your device. We do not use external servers or cloud-based storage for data persistence.

2. Data Ownership

On-Device Storage: All data, including financial records or user configurations, is securely stored on your device. It never leaves your device unless you choose to export it.

Export Options: Our applications provide users with the option to export data to a physical file. This file remains entirely under the user’s control and is never transmitted to any external entity or system.

3. Transparency and User Control

No Hidden Processes: There are no hidden background processes or features designed to extract or analyze user information.

Complete User Autonomy: Users have full control over their data. Any actions regarding data export, deletion, or modification are explicitly user-initiated.

4. Security Measures

While Satoshi Ltd does not store user data on servers or in the cloud, we employ robust encryption and security mechanisms to protect data stored on the device. Users are encouraged to secure their devices with passwords, PINs, or biometric methods to prevent unauthorized access.

5. Data Sharing Policy

Satoshi Ltd does not share, sell, or transmit user data to third parties under any circumstances. This policy applies to all products developed and maintained by Satoshi Ltd.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect improvements in our applications or changes to regulatory requirements. Users will be notified of any significant updates within the application interface.

7. Contact Information

If you have questions, concerns, or require assistance regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@satoshi-ltd.com
