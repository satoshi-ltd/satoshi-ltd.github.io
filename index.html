Our Solutions

C

Clonara

Ethical alternative to personality cloning. Create conversational avatars while always maintaining your authority and total control over the generated AI. Zero data uploading, complete local processing.

S

Split/Pass

Advanced secret splitting system using Shamir's cryptographic algorithms. Split your passwords into 3 shards, requires minimum 2 to reconstruct. Compatible with QR codes and NFC. Military-grade security.

M

Money

Multi-currency and crypto-friendly personal finance manager. Track Bitcoin, Ethereum, and traditional currencies. Helps you become aware of your spending patterns and develop intelligent saving habits.

N

Nutriva

Nutritional analysis through photography using local AI processing. Identifies ingredients, calculates nutritional values and generates healthier recipe alternatives. All processing happens on your device.
