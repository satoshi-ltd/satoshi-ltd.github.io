Main Transformations

Distribution Channels

Advanced Personalization

Operational Sustainability

Generational Differences

Post-Pandemic Evolution

Accelerated recovery: In 2021, OTAs experienced a 97% increase in gross hotel bookings in the US, reaching nearly $41 billion.

Channel rebalancing: By 2023, direct online channels were projected to surpass OTAs again, representing 51% of total online leisure sales.

Emerging Technologies

2025-2026: Web Transformation

Web will remain relevant but transform towards more intuitive interfaces with greater AI integration for personalization and immersive interfaces with virtual reality.

2026-2027: Emerging Channels

Gradual migration towards native mobile applications, messaging platforms, virtual assistants, and systems integrated into broader digital ecosystems.

2027-2030: Omnichannel Era

Complete integration between channels with fluid experiences that allow starting bookings on one channel and completing them on another without context loss.

AI Agent Capabilities

  • Automatic search and filtering based on complex criteria
  • Rate negotiation within established parameters
  • Change and cancellation management without human intervention
  • Integration with digital ecosystems (Alexa, Google Assistant)

  • Experience competition: Differentiation based on digital experience quality
  • Platform strategy: Flexibility to leverage competitive ecosystem
  • Visual focus: Immersive content critical for Gen Z and Millennials
